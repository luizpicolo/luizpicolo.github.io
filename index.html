Professor BTTE / Web Developer

I am a professor (EBTT) at the Federal Institute of Mato Grosso do Sul (IFMS/2018), Brazil, where I work in several teaching modalities, from high school to higher education. I have a degree in History from the Federal University of Mato Grosso do Sul (UFMS/2011) and a Technologist degree in Systems Analysis and Development from IFMS (2015). Additionally, I have a Master's degree in Applied Computing from UFMS (2019).

My current research, as well as the courses I teach, are focused on distributed systems, with a main focus on Decentralized Web and Server-Side programming. However, I have also conducted research involving chatbots for healthcare and software for educational purposes.
