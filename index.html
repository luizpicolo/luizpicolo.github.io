I am a professor (EBTT) at the Federal Institute of Mato Grosso do Sul (IFMS), Brazil, where I work in various teaching modalities from high school to higher education. I am graduated in History by the Federal University of Mato Grosso do Sul (UFMS / 2011) and Technologist in Analysis and Systems Development by the Federal Institute of Mato Grosso do Sul (IFMS / 2015). I am also a master in Applied Computing at UFMS / 2019. My researches, as well as my master's, cover the field of applied computing for both agriculture and for percutaneous, both with an emphasis on IoT (Internet of things). I have been an IFMS professor since 2018.
